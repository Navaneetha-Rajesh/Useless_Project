Cast Your Spell

Available Spells

  • accio: Play a random song
  • sonorus: Increase volume
  • quietus: Decrease volume
  • avada kedavra: Stop music
  • lumos: Switch to light theme
  • nox: Switch to dark theme
  • alohomora: Unlock hidden song
  • expelliarmus: Pause music
  • reducto: Skip to next song
  • muffliato: Mute/unmute
  • wingardium leviosa: Shuffle playlist
  • expecto patronum: Play special song

Invalid spells show a Muggle alert!
